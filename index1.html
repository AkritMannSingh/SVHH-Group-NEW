Our Impact

Continuing the Legacy of Swami Vivekananda

Inspired by the teachings of Swami Vivekananda, our organization was founded in 2010 with a mission to uplift the underprivileged through education, poverty alleviation, and women empowerment.

We believe in the inherent potential of every individual and work to provide opportunities that help people realize their capabilities and contribute positively to society.

Over the past decade, we've impacted over 50,000 lives through our various initiatives across rural and urban areas, focusing on sustainable development and community empowerment.

Our Causes

Education for All

We run free schools, provide scholarships, and offer vocational training to ensure every child and youth has access to quality education regardless of their socioeconomic background.

Poverty Alleviation

Through skill development programs, microfinance initiatives, and sustainable livelihood projects, we empower individuals and families to break the cycle of poverty.

Women Empowerment

We promote gender equality through education, entrepreneurship training, legal awareness, and leadership programs that help women become self-reliant and confident.
